Admission Procedure

TCET Admissions in Under Graduate (UG) & Post Graduate (PG) programme are carried out in the institute as per the norms of Directorate of Technical Education – Maharashtra State. The rules and regulations prescribed by All India Council for Technical Education, Directorate of Technical Education – Maharashtra State and University of Mumbai are applicable.
